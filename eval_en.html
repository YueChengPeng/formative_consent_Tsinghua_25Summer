Research Purpose

We aim to study how an intelligent platform can better support makers in discovering, understanding, and applying academic research in HCI personal fabrication. By evaluating this prototype system, we hope to improve its design and functionality to more effectively address makers’ needs.

Research Process

If you agree to participate, you will use the prototype platform and complete a short task (e.g., searching, browsing, or reviewing papers). Afterward, we will conduct an interview to gather your feedback on usability, effectiveness, and potential improvements. The entire session (prototype use and interview) will be recorded to ensure accurate analysis of your feedback.

Potential Risks or Discomfort

The interview may involve your personal fabrication experiences, understanding and application of academic research, and difficulties or setbacks encountered during replication. If you feel uncomfortable during the interview, you may request to skip questions or terminate the interview.

Benefits of Participation

As appreciation, we will provide 20 USD as interview compensation after the interview. If the research team finds that participants do not meet the research participation requirements or provide false information, the research team reserves the right to suspend compensation. Participants must be at least 18 years old.

Confidentiality of Research Information

All your personal information and interview data will be strictly confidential and used only for this research. In subsequent publications, we will anonymize and desensitize all personal information. Only research team members can access interview records. Relevant government or university departments may conduct compliance reviews of this research, but reviewers also have confidentiality obligations.

In addition, you should strictly keep confidential the specific content of this research, the research details, and all materials generated during the research process, and must not disclose them to any third party.
